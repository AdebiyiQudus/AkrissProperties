Akriss property limited is a one stop property agency located in kent. Its aim is to provide high end accommodation to professionals, students, families. We provide long term guaranteed rent of up to 5 years, and as well as management and maintenance of your property with no work from your end

We Offer Premium Shared Homes

We focus on premium shared homes (HMOs) and fully managed accommodations. Our proficiency and in-depth local understanding allow us to deliver exceptional service to both property owners and renters. We promise landlords rent for 3-5 years. No vacancies. No concealed charges. No commission

We finish cosmetic renovations for free.

Our expert team will perform a complimentary cosmetic renovation* and completely furnish your property to appeal to our upscale professional tenants. This means you gain added value for your property and the assurance that it will be returned to you in excellent condition.

*We understand our clients expect a superior quality of finish, and will allocate additional budget if the required work exceeds that of a standard rental.

We provide ways to buy.

At the conclusion of our agreement, we offer several choices, including the option for Hands Off to buy your property if it fits your long-term objectives. No costs, no intermediaries, and no complications!

How it works.

We make our Landlord onboarding process as fast and efficient as possible.

3-5 year agreements with guaranteed rent

Your rent is guaranteed for the entire term of the management agreement with no commissions or hidden fees.

Free cosmetic refurb completed by our power team

We will carry out a free cosmetic refurb and furnish your property to attract professional tenants. This won't cost you a penny.

Viewings with professional tenants to fill your property

We conduct viewings with our company clients including East Midlands Airport, Bombardier, The Queens Medical and secure the very best professional tenants.

We pay you whilst we find the clients, to minimise your void period.

Management and maintenance of your property

Hands-Off take over all of the management and maintenance of the property to give you a truly "hands-off" experience.

End of agreement purchase options

At the end of the agreement, you will have three options: i. To extend the contract ii. Have the property returned to you (obviously in fantastic condition) iii. Hands-Off can arrange the purchase of your property
